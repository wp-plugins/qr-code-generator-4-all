== Description ==

<p>With our free plugin QR Code Generator 4 All we give all website owners the ability to integrate a comfortable
QR code generator in your website with a shortcode snippet. So you provide your customers a real added value.</p>
<p>QR Codes (Quick Response Codes) are coded graphics, that can be read by current smartphones
and tablets with the appropriate software. They form a bridge between the real and the virtual world.
Nowadays it's hard to find advertisements without this code. With one scan you are directed to a website,
a product video or a download link in the App Store. Receive immediately information or the content you
want without having to type a link. Complete Business cards are imported within seconds.
Even pre-formulated, pre-addressed mail and SMS as well as free text messages can be transmitted.
With the enormous range of practical applications QR Codes are continuously gaining importance.</p>

The plugin provides the following features:

* Easy installation and integration
* Generates QR Codes for business cards, links, SMS, free text, phone numbers and emails
* Custom-QR-Codes including your logo
* Intuitive operation
* Professional design
* Colors, spacing and fonts are customizable
* Multilingual. Both the user interface and the configuration page in the administration area are available in the following languages​​: German, English, Dutch and Japanese. Own translations can be added easily.
* Shortcodes for dynamically generated QR Codes on your webpages

== Installation ==

<ol>
<li>Click "Install Now" in search results row</li>
<li>Click "OK" to accept plugin installation</li>
<li>Once installed, click the link "Activate Plugin"</li>
<li>Go to Settings->QR Generator 4 All. Here you can create QR Codes and learn how to integrate them. 
</ol>
